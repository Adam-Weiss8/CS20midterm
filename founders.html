About Us

Jake, Nick, Adam, and Rodrigo all share a deep love for the wilderness, and their passion for nature led them to start a company aimed at protecting the woodlands surrounding Tufts. Together, they recognized the importance of preserving these natural areas and the crucial role they play in the local ecosystem. Their company works towards promoting sustainable practices and protecting wildlife habitats, while also educating others on the importance of conservation efforts. Through their dedication and hard work, Jake, Nick, Adam, and Rodrigo have become advocates for the protection of the environment, inspiring others to follow in their footsteps and work towards a more sustainable future for us all.
