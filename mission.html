Sustainability

We use eco-friendly materials, reducing waste by using scrap material, and minimizing their carbon footprint by donating 10% of proceeds to WWF.

Conservation

Protecting natural habitats and preserving biodiversity is done by supporting conservation efforts and reducing human impact on the environment.

Education

By educating customers and the wider public about environmental issues and the importance of protecting wildlife, we can raise awareness and inspire positive change.
